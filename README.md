## Installation

1. Download the latest release zip
2. Extract `FS25_RoleplayInvoices` folder into your FS25 mods directory:
   - Windows: `Documents/My Games/FarmingSimulator2025/mods/`
3. Enable the mod in-game

## Usage

- Press **F7** to open the phone
- Click **Invoices** to view inbox/outbox
- Click **+ Create Invoice** in the Outbox tab to send an invoice
- Click any invoice row to view details and pay or mark as paid
- Click outside the phone or press **F7** again to close

## Multiplayer

Best experienced on a **dedicated server**. Player-hosted multiplayer functionality may be limited until a future update adds full network event support.
